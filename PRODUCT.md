# PRODUCT — CE Credits Online

> Target strategy resolved by `stardust:direct` (Mode A, brand-faithful).
> Phrase: "faithful redesign, modernized — single variant, no gate."

## Register

`brand` — a trust-forward, teacher-facing education brand. Marketing/editorial,
not a tool or dashboard.

## Users

- **K-12 classroom teachers** earning accredited PD credit for license
  recertification or salary-scale advancement — the primary audience.
- **District / ESD administrators** routing staff to pre-approved courses
  (the per-state/district "how-to-earn" pages serve them).
- **Prospective educators** browsing the catalog and the classroom-tips blog.

## Product Purpose

Help K-12 teachers earn accredited, graduate-level professional-development
credit through flexible, self-paced online courses. Scope: course catalog,
credit-pathway guidance by state/district, and a classroom-practice blog —
reproduced faithfully as an editorial Edge Delivery site.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, established, warm, academic-but-approachable. Twenty-plus years
(founded 2001), woman-owned, "professional development should be worth a
teacher's time." Leads with accreditation/legitimacy, flexibility, real
classroom applicability, and heavy authentic social proof (94% of 20,000+
educators; hundreds of thousands served; teacher testimonials). The voice is
teacher-to-teacher, never corporate-sterile and never startup-loud.

## Anti-references

- **Generic 2026 SaaS silhouette** — no centered hero + dual primary-blue CTA
  + three-icon feature row. (Modernize ≠ template.)
- **MOOC marketplace** (Udemy/Coursera) — curated and accreditation-anchored,
  not an infinite course bazaar.
- **Corporate LMS sterility** — stays warm and human.
- **Storefront-first chrome** — the EDS target is editorial; live cart/account
  furniture is dropped.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust over hype** — cream ground, forest-green accent, generous calm;
   credibility cues (accreditation, stats, testimonials) lead every page.
2. **Reproduce, don't out-author** — content is the source's real content,
   verbatim; the redesign modernizes surface, never invents sections or facts.
3. **Wayfinding by credit type** — collections and "how-to-earn" pages route a
   teacher to exactly the credit their state/district accepts.
4. **Social proof as conversion** — for a credential-buying audience, the
   testimonials and the 94%/20,000 stat are the hero of the trust argument.
5. **Mixed-case calm** — reserve uppercase for short eyebrows and CTAs; section
   headings read mixed-case and current.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: forest-green primary on cream/white meets AA for large text and
UI; body text uses ink `#2D2D2D` on cream for AAA-leaning contrast. Proper
heading hierarchy (one h1/page), alt text on all real imagery, reduced-motion
honored on any animated hero.
