Collection: NYC DOE ASPDP +30 Salary Differential Courses

NYC DOE teachers — CE Credits Online courses are approved by ASPDP and count toward your +30 salary differential. Our courses are fully online and self-paced, and also qualify for CTLE hours toward your New York certificate renewal.

CE Credits Online courses are listed on the NYC DOE ASPDP approved courses page — log in with your NYC DOE credentials to verify.

Our courses are accepted for ASPDP P-credits toward your +30 salary differential, A+ credits, and CTLE hours. Complete your course by the term deadline and we enter your grade into the ASPDP system — ASPDP then processes your credits.

Current ASPDP Term Dates

You can enroll in your CE Credits Online course at any time — but you must register in the ASPDP system before the registration deadline to receive credit. Enrolling with CE Credits Online does not automatically register you with ASPDP.

ASPDP charges a separate registration fee — $125 for a 3-credit course and $45 for a 1-credit course. Maximum of 12 credits per term.

How to enroll:

Select your course, add it to your cart, and check out. Once you successfully complete your course by the term deadline, we enter your grade into the ASPDP system — ASPDP then processes your credits. Remember to also register each course in the ASPDP system before the registration deadline.

For the full process including step-by-step ASPDP registration instructions, visit our NYC DOE ASPDP how-to page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
