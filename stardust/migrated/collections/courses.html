Collection: Online Professional Development Courses for K-12 Teachers

CE Credits Online offers fully online, self-paced professional development courses for K-12 teachers. Complete a course and receive a Certificate of Completion — or add university graduate credits at checkout for an official transcript from one of our accredited university partners.

Our courses are accepted for CEUs, Salary Points, In-Service Points, Lane Credits, Clock Hours, and Graduate-Level University Credits depending on your state and district. Check the Credits Tab on any course page for details specific to your location.

Not sure if your district will accept the credits? Download the course syllabus — you'll find the link on the course page below the Add to Cart button — and share it with your professional development department before purchasing.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need a Certificate of Completion only? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details on our regionally accredited partners.
