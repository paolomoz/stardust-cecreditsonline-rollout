Collection: Chicago Public Schools Lane Credits, Clock Hours and CPDUs Courses for K-12 Teachers

CE Credits Online courses are approved by Chicago Public Schools for Lane Credits, Clock Hours, and CPDUs, and are available on the CPS Learning Hub. Our courses are fully online and self-paced.

Earn Lane Credits and clock hours for CPS salary advancement.

CPS courses are processed through the CPS Learning Hub. Complete your course, and we submit your transcript directly to CPS — no extra steps needed.

Already completed all courses on the Learning Hub?Illinois collection courses with university credits can also be submitted to CPS for Lane Credits. Learn more →

Questions? See our How to Earn CPS Lane Credits guide, including CPS Learning Hub class codes and FAQs.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need Lane Credits and clock hours? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.
