Collection: New York State CTLE Courses for K-12 Teachers

CE Credits Online is an NYSED-approved CTLE sponsor. Our courses are fully online and self-paced — earn CTLE hours toward your New York certificate renewal, with language acquisition courses available to meet that specific requirement.

CE Credits Online is recognized by NYSED's Office of Teaching Initiatives as an approved CTLE sponsor (ID #23657). To verify, visit the NYSED Approved CTLE Sponsors list and search for CE Credits Online.

Courses are accepted for CTLE hours, Graduate Credit, and Graduate Level Professional Development credits. We offer courses in language acquisition, social-emotional learning, technology, and more.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need CTLE hours only? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.

For the full process visit our How to Earn New York State CTLE Hours page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
