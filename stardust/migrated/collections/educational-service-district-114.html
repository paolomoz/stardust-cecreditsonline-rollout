Collection: Washington State ESD 114 Professional Development Courses

CE Credits Online courses are approved by ESD 114 for Washington State clock hours and graduate-level professional development credit. Our courses are fully online and self-paced — complete your course, then purchase your clock hours through pdEnroller.

Washington State clock hours are purchased separately through pdEnroller at $3 per clock hour — this fee is paid directly to ESD 114 and is separate from your CE Credits Online course purchase.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need clock hours? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown, then purchase your clock hours through pdEnroller after completing your course.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.

For the full process visit our How to Earn ESD 114 Clock Hours page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
