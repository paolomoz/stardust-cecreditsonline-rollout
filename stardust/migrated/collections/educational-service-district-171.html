Collection: Washington State ESD 171 Professional Development Courses

CE Credits Online courses are approved by ESD 171 (Olympic Educational Service District) for Washington State clock hours and graduate-level professional development credit. Our courses are fully online and self-paced.

After completing your course, submit your Certificate of Completion to ESD 171 at registration@ncesd.org. ESD 171 will enter your attendance into pdEnroller — you'll receive an email to complete a short survey and pay the $3 per clock hour fee by credit card. Clock hours are then automatically recorded to your pdEnroller transcript.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need clock hours? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown, then follow the pdEnroller process after completing your course.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.

For the full process visit our How to Earn ESD 171 Clock Hours page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
