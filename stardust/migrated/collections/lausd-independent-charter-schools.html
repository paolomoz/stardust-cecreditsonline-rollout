Collection: LAUSD Independent Charter School Salary Point Courses

CE Credits Online courses are available to LAUSD independent charter school teachers at special discounted pricing. Our courses are fully online and self-paced.

CE Credits Online courses are available for LAUSD Independent Charter Schools salary points. Independent charter school teachers receive a Certificate of Completion to submit directly to their school's professional development office for salary point processing.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need a certificate of completion for salary points? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.

For the full process visit our How to Earn Charter School Salary Points page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
