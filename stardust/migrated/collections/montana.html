Collection: Montana OPI Renewal Unit Courses for K-12 Teachers

CE Credits Online is a Montana State approved provider for OPI renewal units. Our courses are fully online and self-paced — earn renewal units toward your Montana teaching license renewal on your own schedule.

Montana requires a Registration and Verification Form for each course completed for OPI renewal units. After you finish your course, contact us and we'll send you the form with our portion already completed — you fill in your sections and submit it with your license renewal application.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need OPI renewal units only? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.

For the full process visit our How to Earn Montana OPI Renewal Units page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
