Collection: Pasadena PUSD Salary Credit Courses for K-12 Teachers

CE Credits Online is recognized by Pasadena Unified School District as a partner for web-based professional development. Our courses are eligible for PUSD salary credits and university units — fully online and self-paced.

PUSD awards 1 salary credit for every 15 hours of professional development completed outside of regular work hours. Complete your course, then submit the PUSD Salary Credit Pre-Approval form along with your Certificate of Completion.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need salary credits only? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.

For the full process visit our How to Earn PUSD Salary Credits page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
