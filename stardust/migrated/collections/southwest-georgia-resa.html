Collection: Southwest Georgia RESA PLU Courses for K-12 Teachers

CE Credits Online is partnered with Southwest Georgia RESA. Our courses are fully online and self-paced, and count toward your PLUs and recertification needs through the RESA partnership.

PLUs are calculated based on course hours — 15 hours = 1.5 PLUs, 30 hours = 3 PLUs, 45 hours = 4.5 PLUs. Complete your course, receive your Certificate of Completion, and email it to Southwest Georgia RESA to receive your PLUs.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need PLUs only? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.

For the full process visit our How to Earn Southwest Georgia RESA PLUs page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
