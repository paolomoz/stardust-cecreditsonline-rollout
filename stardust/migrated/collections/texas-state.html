Collection: Texas CPE Courses for K-12 Teachers

CE Credits Online is a TEA-approved CPE provider offering fully online, self-paced courses for Texas K-12 teachers. Our courses earn CPE credits toward Texas State License Renewal and Graduate-level Professional Development Credit — all materials included, complete on your own schedule, 24/7.

We are a TEA-approved CPE provider — Texas Approved CPE Provider #500-494 since 2004. Our provider name is CPE Credits Online.

Courses cover a wide range of topics and are eligible for both Graduate-level Professional Development Credit and CPE Credits towards Texas State License Renewal.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need a Certificate of Completion for your Texas CPE records? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details on our regionally accredited partners.

For the full process visit our How to Earn Texas CPE Credits page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
