Collection: Wyoming PTSB Professional Development Courses For K-12 Teachers

CE Credits Online is a Wyoming PTSB approved provider. Our courses are fully online and self-paced — earn renewal units toward your Wyoming teaching license renewal on your own schedule.

Complete your course and receive your Certificate of Completion. CE Credits Online reports your completion directly to WY PTSB within 10 days — you don't have to do anything.

Choosing your enrollment type:

  • Need PTSB renewal units only? Choose Course Only in the Type dropdown.
  • Need an official university transcript? Choose University Credits. See our University Partners page for details.

For the full process visit our How to Earn Wyoming PTSB Renewal Units page. Questions? Check our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.
