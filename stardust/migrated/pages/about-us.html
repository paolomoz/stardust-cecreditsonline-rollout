About

For more than 20 years, CE Credits Online has been helping K-12 teachers grow — on their schedule, at their own pace, and in ways that actually matter in the classroom.

Founded in 2001, CE Credits Online is a woman-owned company built around one simple idea: professional development should be worth a teacher's time. Our graduate-level courses are completely online and asynchronous, so you learn when it works for you — no commutes, no set schedules, no sitting through content you already know.

Every CE Credits Online course is eligible for graduate-level credit from regionally accredited universities, and hundreds of thousands of educators have trusted us over the past two decades. School districts across the country — including Los Angeles Unified School District, the New York City Department of Education, and Chicago Public Schools have made our courses part of their professional development programs.

We focus on what teachers tell us they actually need: differentiation and technology integration, support for ELL and Special Needs students, and real-world approaches to classroom management. Every course is research-based, immediately applicable, and designed by educators who understand what you're walking into every morning.

You'll also never go it alone, we work with you the whole way. Our courses include multiple assessments, peer interaction, and one-on-one feedback from moderators who hold a master's degree or higher in education. 96% of teachers who've taken a CE Credits Online course say they'd take another — and 91% say it made a positive impact on how they teach.

We're proud of those numbers. But honestly, we're more proud of the teachers behind them
