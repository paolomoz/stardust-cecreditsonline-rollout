Quick Answer

To earn CPS Lane Credits or Clock Hours / CPDUs, you must:

  • Purchase your course through CE Credits Online
  • Register your course in the CPS Learning Hub using the class code from the class codes table below
  • Complete your coursework and the course evaluation in CPS Learning Hub

Both steps are required and can be completed in either order. Your class code is listed on each course page — you'll need it when registering in CPS Learning Hub.

Already completed all CE Credits Online courses on the CPS Learning Hub? You can still earn Lane Credits through our university partners.

  • Browse any course from our Illinois collection and add university credits at purchase
  • An official transcript will be issued by our university partner and can be submitted to Chicago Public Schools for Lane Credits
  • This path does not go through the CPS Learning Hub

How to Get Your CPS Credits

Lane Credits

After you complete your course:

  • We will email you a certificate of completion
  • Complete the course evaluation in your CPS Learning Hub account — we will then mark your status as complete
  • When filling out your Lane Credit application, list the class title and class code from your course page. You do not need an official transcript.

Clock Hours / CPDUs

After you complete your course:

  • We will email you a certificate of completion
  • Your Certificate of Completion is required when submitting your Clock Hours to ISBE

University Credits

  • If you didn't purchase university credits when you successfully completed, university credits may be requested by emailing support@cecreditsonline.org
  • CE Credits Online will forward the necessary documentation to the university of your choice for an official transcript

CPS Learning Hub Class Codes

When registering in CPS Learning Hub, use the class code for each course you are taking. Enter the code exactly as shown below.

CPS Lane Credit & CPDU Program Rules

  • You must complete both CE Credits Online enrollment and CPS Learning Hub registration
  • Each course has a unique class code — find it listed on the course page
  • The course evaluation in CPS Learning Hub must be completed to receive Lane Credit
  • Your Certificate of Completion is required for Clock Hour / CPDU submission to ISBE
  • You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course

CPS Lane Credit FAQs

Do I need to register in CPS Learning Hub before purchasing my course?

No. You can purchase your course through CE Credits Online first and register in CPS Learning Hub afterward. Just make sure to register before you complete your course so your completion is recorded correctly.

Where do I find the class code for my course?

The class code for each course is listed in the class codes table on this page. You'll enter it in the CPS Learning Hub when registering your course.

Do I need an official transcript for Lane Credits?

No. For Lane Credits, you only need the class title and class code from your course page. An official transcript is not required.

What do I need for Clock Hour / CPDU submission?

You'll need the Certificate of Completion we email you after finishing your course. Submit it to ISBE for Clock Hour / CPDU credit.

Can I earn both Lane Credits and Clock Hours from the same course?

Yes. The same course can count toward Lane Credits and Clock Hours / CPDUs depending on how you apply for credit.

How do I request university credits?

Email support@cecreditsonline.org after completing your course. We will forward the necessary documentation to the university of your choice.

How to Enroll

CPS courses require registration in two places — CE Credits Online and the CPS Learning Hub. Both steps are required to receive credit.

Step 1: Register in CPS Learning Hub

  1. Go to the CPS Learning Hub and sign in
  2. Find your course using the class code from the class codes table above
  3. Register for the course in CPS Learning Hub

Step 2: Purchase Through CE Credits Online

  1. Go to our Chicago Public Schools courses page
  2. Add your course to cart and check out
  3. You will be asked to log in or create an account
  4. If you have a discount code, enter it at the end of checkout
  5. Upon purchase you will receive an email receipt and can log in to your course at eClassroom Sign in

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course.
