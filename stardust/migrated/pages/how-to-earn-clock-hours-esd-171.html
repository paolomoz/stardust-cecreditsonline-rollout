How to get your ESD 171 Clock Hours through Olympic Educational Service District 171

CE Credits Online courses are approved by ESD 171 for Washington State Clock Hours and Graduate-Level Professional Development Credits.

CE Credits Online offers self-paced online professional development courses approved by ESD 171 for Washington State Clock Hours and Graduate-Level Professional Development Credits. Our research-based courses include all materials and facilitation — complete them on your own schedule, 24/7.

Quick Answer

To earn ESD 171 Clock Hours with CE Credits Online:

  • Successfully complete your course and receive your Certificate of Completion
  • Submit your certificate to ESD 171 at registration@ncesd.org
  • ESD 171 enters your attendance into pdEnroller — you'll receive an email to process your clock hour request
  • Complete a short survey and pay the $3 per clock hour fee by credit card
  • Your clock hours are automatically recorded to your pdEnroller transcript

Note: the $3 per hour fee is paid directly to ESD 171 through pdEnroller — it is not part of your CE Credits Online purchase.

Looking for STEM courses? ESD 171 has approved specific courses for STEM. When browsing our ESD 171 courses page, use the subject filter to find approved STEM courses. Some teachers are required to complete courses in this subject — check with your district if unsure.

How to Get Your ESD 171 Clock Hours

Once you successfully complete your course, we'll email you your Certificate of Completion. Here's what to do next:

  1. Submit your Certificate of Completion to ESD 171 at registration@ncesd.org
  2. Once your attendance has been entered into pdEnroller, you'll receive an email with a link to process your clock hour request
  3. Complete a short survey and pay the $3 per clock hour fee by credit card
  4. Your clock hours are automatically recorded to your pdEnroller transcript

To print your transcript, log in to pdEnroller, click the Clock Hours dropdown (upper right), select My Clock Hours, and click the blue Download Transcripts button.

For clock hour or pdEnroller assistance, contact ESD 171 at registration@ncesd.org.

University Credits

If you need an official university transcript, you can add university credits when you purchase. Once you complete your course, we take care of the rest — we send all the required documentation directly to your chosen university, and they'll issue your official transcript and email you instructions for accessing it. Your transcript will include the course name, course number, number of credits, and your grade.

Graduate-level credits are available through Adams State University, Augustana University, Valley City State University, and Cal Poly Humboldt. Visit our University Partners page to learn more.

ESD 171 Clock Hour Program Rules

  • Clock hours cost $3 per hour — paid directly to ESD 171 through pdEnroller, not through CE Credits Online
  • You must submit your Certificate of Completion to ESD 171 before your clock hours can be processed
  • ESD 171 has approved specific courses for STEM — use the subject filter when browsing courses
  • You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course
  • University credits must be added at purchase if needed

ESD 171 Clock Hour FAQs

How much do ESD 171 clock hours cost?

Clock hours cost $3 per hour and are paid directly to ESD 171 through pdEnroller by credit card. This fee is separate from your CE Credits Online course purchase.

What do I do after I complete my course?

Email your Certificate of Completion to ESD 171 at registration@ncesd.org. Once your attendance is entered into pdEnroller, you'll receive an email with instructions to process your clock hour request and pay the $3 per hour fee.

How do I access my clock hour transcript?

Log in to pdEnroller, click the Clock Hours dropdown in the upper right, select My Clock Hours, and click the blue Download Transcripts button. Your clock hours are automatically recorded once processed.

Which courses are approved for STEM?

ESD 171 has approved specific CE Credits Online courses for STEM. Use the subject filter on our ESD 171 courses page to find them. Contact your district if you are unsure which subject areas you need.

Do I need an official transcript for ESD 171 Clock Hours?

No. Your Certificate of Completion is what you submit to ESD 171 for clock hours — no university transcript is required. If you also need a university transcript for other purposes, you can add university credits at purchase.

How long do I have to complete my course?

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from the date of purchase to complete your course at your own pace.

Who do I contact for clock hour questions?

For clock hour processing or pdEnroller questions, contact ESD 171 at registration@ncesd.org. For questions about your course or enrollment, contact CE Credits Online.

How to Enroll

  1. Go to our ESD 171 approved courses page
  2. Browse courses — if you need STEM approved courses, use the subject filter to find them
  3. On the course page, use the Type dropdown: Course Only — includes your Certificate of Completion, which you'll submit to ESD 171 for clock hours University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.
  4. Add the course to your cart and check out
  5. You will be asked to log in or create an account
  6. If you have a discount code, enter it at the end of checkout
  7. Upon purchase you will receive an email receipt and can log in to your course at eClassroom Sign in
  • Course Only — includes your Certificate of Completion, which you'll submit to ESD 171 for clock hours
  • University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course.
