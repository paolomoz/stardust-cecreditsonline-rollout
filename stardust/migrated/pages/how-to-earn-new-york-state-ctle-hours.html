How to get your CTLE Hours

After you have successfully completed your course:

  • You will receive a CTLE Certificate of Completion for your records. This certificate includes the name of the course, the number of professional development hours completed, and our CTLE ID Number.
  • The Certificate of Completion is designed as a record that verifies that a professional development course was taken and passed.

University Credits

If you need an official university transcript, you can add university credits when you purchase. Once you complete your course, we take care of the rest — we send all the required documentation directly to your chosen university, and they'll issue your official transcript and email you instructions for accessing it. Your transcript will include the course name, course number, number of credits, and your grade.

Graduate-level credits are available through Adams State University, Augustana University, Valley City State University, and Cal Poly Humboldt. Visit our University Partners page to learn more.

New York CTLE FAQs

What is the Type dropdown when I select a course?

The Type dropdown lets you choose your enrollment option. Course Only gives you a CTLE Certificate of Completion for your New York renewal records. University Credits adds an official university transcript from one of our partner universities. For CTLE hours only, Course Only is all you need.

What does my CTLE Certificate of Completion include?

Your CTLE Certificate of Completion includes the name of the course, the number of professional development hours completed, and our CTLE ID Number. This is your documentation for the NYSED renewal process — keep it for your records.

Which courses meet the language acquisition requirement?

We offer six courses eligible for the language acquisition requirement — they are listed in the Courses Eligible for Language Acquisition Requirement section above. Check with NYSED or your district to confirm the specific hours required for your certificate type.

Does CE Credits Online submit my CTLE records to NYSED?

No — your CTLE Certificate of Completion is your documentation for the renewal process. Certificate renewal is completed directly through NYSED using your certificate as documentation.

Can I add university credits after I purchase a course?

University credits are easiest to add at purchase using the Type dropdown on the course page. If you completed a course and later find out you need university credits, contact us within 30 days of completion and we can help.

How long do I have to complete a course?

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from your purchase date to complete your course. All courses are self-paced — no scheduled sessions or live requirements.

Who do I contact with questions?

For questions about your course or enrollment, check out our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.

How to Enroll

  1. Go to our New York State CTLE courses page and select a course you'd like to take.
  2. On the course page, use the Type dropdown: Course Only — includes your CTLE Certificate of Completion for New York renewal records University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.
  3. Add the course to your cart.
  4. When you are ready to check out, go to your cart and click "Check Out".
  5. You will be asked to log into your account or create a new account.
  6. Follow the enrollment and payment prompts. If you have a discount code, enter it at the end of the enrollment process.
  7. Upon purchase you will receive an email receipt and can log in to your course at eClassroom Sign in.
  • Course Only — includes your CTLE Certificate of Completion for New York renewal records
  • University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course.

If you haven't already done so, please add the required information to your My Account page before completing your course. This only needs to be done once and is needed for your CTLE Certificate of Completion.
