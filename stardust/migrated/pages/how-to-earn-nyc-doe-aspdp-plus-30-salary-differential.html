Quick Answer

To earn NYC DOE ASPDP credits, you must:

  • Enroll in your course through CE Credits Online
  • Register your course in the ASPDP system before the deadline
  • Successfully complete all coursework before the end of the ASPDP term

Both steps are easy and can be done in either order. Just don't miss the ASPDP registration deadline.

Important: Enrolling in a course does not automatically register you with ASPDP. You must complete both steps separately.

How NYC DOE ASPDP Credits Work with CE Credits Online

Step 1: Enroll in Your Course

Purchase your courses with CE Credits Online. You can enroll before ASPDP registration opens or at any time before registration closes. Courses do not begin until the official ASPDP term start date.

Step 2: Register in NYC DOE ASPDP

Register your course in the NYC DOE ASPDP system before the registration deadline. Our courses are in the ASPDP course catalog — use the Education Partner drop-down menu and select CE Credits Online.

Step 3: Complete Your Course

Complete your coursework during the ASPDP term (see dates above). You must complete your courses before the term end date to receive credit.

Step 4: Receive Credit

Your credits are validated within 2 weeks after the term ends. Once you have been "Validated for Credit" on the ASPDP site, you will be able to print your Credit Transcript. Go to "Show Enrolled Courses" on the ASPDP site and select the "View Print Transcripts" tab.

NYC DOE ASPDP Program Rules & Limits

  • You must complete both CE Credits Online enrollment and NYC DOE ASPDP registration — steps can be done in either order
  • ASPDP registration must be completed before the registration deadline
  • All coursework must be completed before the term end date
  • ASPDP charges a separate registration fee per course ($125 for 3-credit, $45 for 1-credit)
  • Maximum of 12 credits per term (typically four 3-credit courses)
  • ASPDP terms run Fall, Spring, and Summer

NYC DOE ASPDP FAQs

For official NYC DOE ASPDP policies, visit the NYC Public Schools FAQ page.

Do I need to register in NYC DOE ASPDP before starting my course?

No. You can enroll in your course with CE Credits Online before ASPDP registration opens. However, you must register for your course with ASPDP before the registration deadline to receive credit.

When can I start my coursework?

Coursework begins on the official ASPDP term start date. Even if you enroll early, you will not begin your course until the term begins.

Do I have to enroll in both CE Credits Online and NYC DOE ASPDP?

Yes. To receive credit, you must enroll in your course through CE Credits Online and register for the course in the ASPDP system. Both steps must be completed before the registration deadline.

How long does it take to receive NYC DOE ASPDP credit?

Credits are typically validated within 2 weeks after the ASPDP term ends. You can then view your transcript in the ASPDP system.

Do I have to pay NYC DOE ASPDP separately?

Yes. ASPDP charges a separate registration fee — $125 for a 3-credit course and $45 for a 1-credit course.

What happens if I miss the NYC DOE ASPDP registration deadline?

If you do not register for your course with ASPDP before the deadline, you will not receive credit for that term.

How many credits can I take per NYC DOE ASPDP term?

Teachers can take up to 12 credits per term, typically equivalent to four 3-credit courses.

Where can I find official NYC DOE ASPDP policies?

For additional details, refer to the official NYC DOE ASPDP website.
