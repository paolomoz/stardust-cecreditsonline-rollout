CE Credits Online and PUSD

CE Credits Online is recognized by Pasadena Unified School District as a partner for online professional development. Our courses are eligible for PUSD salary credits and university units.

To verify, visit the PUSD Professional Development for Employees page and open the "CE Credits Online Courses Expanded!" section.

According to PUSD, participation in uncompensated professional development outside of regular work hours may be eligible for salary credit. 15 hours is equivalent to 1 salary credit.

  • 15 hours = 1 salary credit
  • 30 hours = 2 salary credits
  • 45 hours = 3 salary credits

How to Get Your PUSD Salary Credits

Once you successfully complete your course, we'll email you a Certificate of Completion. Here's how to submit it for salary credit processing:

  1. Download and complete the PUSD Salary Credit Pre-Approval form.
  2. Submit the completed form along with your Certificate of Completion for salary credit processing.

Not sure if a specific course will be approved? PUSD recommends submitting the Pre-Approval form before completing the work. The form can also be used to submit your hours for processing after completion.

University Credits

If you need an official university transcript, you can add university credits when you purchase. Once you complete your course, we take care of the rest — we send all the required documentation directly to your chosen university, and they'll issue your official transcript and email you instructions for accessing it. Your transcript will include the course name, course number, number of credits, and your grade.

Graduate-level credits are available through Adams State University, Augustana University, Valley City State University, and Cal Poly Humboldt. Visit our University Partners page to learn more.

PUSD Salary Credit FAQs

How many salary credits will I earn?

PUSD awards 1 salary credit for every 15 hours of uncompensated professional development completed outside of regular work hours. The number of hours in each course is listed on the course page — check the Credits tab for details.

What is the Salary Credit Pre-Approval form?

The PUSD Salary Credit Pre-Approval form is used both to verify that a course is eligible for salary credit before you complete it, and to submit your hours for processing afterward. Submit it along with your Certificate of Completion once your course is done.

What is the Type dropdown when I select a course?

The Type dropdown lets you choose your enrollment option. Course Only gives you a Certificate of Completion to submit with your Pre-Approval form for PUSD salary credits. University Credits adds an official university transcript from one of our partner universities. Check with your professional development office if you're unsure which you need.

What does my Certificate of Completion include?

Your Certificate of Completion includes the name of the course and the number of professional development hours completed. This is what you submit along with your PUSD Salary Credit Pre-Approval form for processing.

Does CE Credits Online submit my salary credit documentation to PUSD?

No — CE Credits Online provides your Certificate of Completion once your course is complete. Submitting the Salary Credit Pre-Approval form along with that certificate to PUSD is the final step in the process.

Can I add university credits after I purchase a course?

University credits are easiest to add at purchase using the Type dropdown on the course page. If you completed a course and later find out you need university credits, contact us within 45 days of completion and we can help.

How long do I have to complete a course?

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from your purchase date to complete your course. All courses are self-paced — no scheduled sessions or live requirements.

Who do I contact with questions about salary credit processing?

For questions about salary credit processing, contact PUSD's professional development office directly. For questions about your course or enrollment, check out our FAQs or email support@cecreditsonline.org.

How to Enroll

  1. Go to our PUSD courses page and select a course you'd like to take.
  2. On the course page, use the Type dropdown: Course Only — includes your Certificate of Completion for PUSD salary credit processing University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.
  3. Add the course to your cart.
  4. When you are ready to check out, go to your cart and click "Check Out".
  5. You will be asked to log into your account or create a new account.
  6. Follow the enrollment and payment prompts. If you have a discount code, enter it at the end of the enrollment process.
  7. Upon purchase you will receive an email receipt and can log in to your course at eClassroom Sign in.
  • Course Only — includes your Certificate of Completion for PUSD salary credit processing
  • University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course. After completing your course, submit the PUSD Salary Credit Pre-Approval form along with your Certificate of Completion for processing.
