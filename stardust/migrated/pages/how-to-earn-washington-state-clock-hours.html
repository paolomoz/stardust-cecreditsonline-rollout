How to Earn Washington State Clock Hours for Teacher License Renewal with CE Credits Online

CE Credits Online courses earn Washington State Clock Hours for teacher license renewal and professional development.

CE Credits Online offers self-paced online professional development courses that earn Washington State Clock Hours toward teacher license renewal. Our research-based courses include all materials and facilitation — complete them on your own schedule, 24/7.

Quick Answer

To earn Washington State Clock Hours with CE Credits Online:

  • Successfully complete your course and receive your Certificate of Completion
  • Submit your certificate to registrar@oesd.wednet.edu
  • Once your attendance is entered into pdEnroller, you'll receive an email to process your clock hour request
  • Complete a short survey and pay the $3 per clock hour fee by credit card
  • Your clock hours are automatically recorded to your pdEnroller transcript

Note: the $3 per hour fee is paid directly through pdEnroller — it is not part of your CE Credits Online purchase.

Looking for Equity courses? ESD 114 has approved specific courses for the Equity subject area. When browsing our Washington State courses page, use the subject filter to find approved Equity courses. Check with your district if you are unsure whether you need an Equity-approved course.

How to Get Your Washington State Clock Hours

Once you successfully complete your course, we'll email you your Certificate of Completion. Here's what to do next:

  1. Submit your Certificate of Completion to registrar@oesd.wednet.edu
  2. Once your attendance has been entered into pdEnroller, you'll receive an email with a link to process your clock hour request
  3. Complete a short survey and pay the $3 per clock hour fee by credit card
  4. Your clock hours are automatically recorded to your pdEnroller transcript

To print your transcript, log in to pdEnroller, click the Clock Hours dropdown (upper right), select My Clock Hours, and click the blue Download Transcripts button.

For clock hour or pdEnroller assistance, contact Heather Archut at harchut@oesd114.org.

University Credits

If you need an official university transcript, you can add university credits when you purchase. Once you complete your course, we take care of the rest — we send all the required documentation directly to your chosen university, and they'll issue your official transcript and email you instructions for accessing it. Your transcript will include the course name, course number, number of credits, and your grade.

Graduate-level credits are available through Adams State University, Augustana University, Valley City State University, and Cal Poly Humboldt. Visit our University Partners page to learn more.

Washington State Clock Hour Program Rules

  • Clock hours cost $3 per hour — paid through pdEnroller, not through CE Credits Online
  • You must submit your Certificate of Completion before your clock hours can be processed
  • ESD 114 has approved specific courses for the Equity subject area — use the subject filter when browsing courses
  • You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course
  • University credits must be added at purchase if needed

Washington State Clock Hour FAQs

How much do Washington State clock hours cost?

Clock hours cost $3 per hour and are paid through pdEnroller by credit card. This fee is separate from your CE Credits Online course purchase.

Can I use these courses for salary advancement?

Many Washington State teachers take our courses for salary advancement in addition to license renewal. Whether courses count toward salary advancement varies by school and district — check with your professional development department to confirm eligibility before purchasing.

Where do I send my Certificate of Completion?

Submit your Certificate of Completion to registrar@oesd.wednet.edu. Once your attendance is entered into pdEnroller, you'll receive an email to process your clock hour request.

How do I access my clock hour transcript?

Log in to pdEnroller, click the Clock Hours dropdown in the upper right, select My Clock Hours, and click the blue Download Transcripts button. Your clock hours are automatically recorded once processed.

Which courses are approved for Equity?

ESD 114 has approved specific CE Credits Online courses for the Equity subject area (1 course). Use the subject filter on our Washington State courses page to find it. Contact your district if you are unsure whether you need an Equity-approved course.

Do I need an official transcript for Washington State Clock Hours?

No. Your Certificate of Completion is what you submit for clock hours — no university transcript is required. If you also need a university transcript for other purposes, you can add university credits at purchase.

Can I use these courses for Washington State License Renewal?

Yes. Through our ESD partnerships, CE Credits Online courses earn Washington State Clock Hours that count toward teacher license renewal. Check with your district or the Washington State Professional Educator Standards Board (PESB) if you have questions about specific requirements.

How long do I have to complete my course?

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from the date of purchase to complete your course at your own pace.

Who do I contact for clock hour questions?

For clock hour processing or pdEnroller questions, contact Heather Archut at harchut@oesd114.org. For questions about your course or enrollment, contact CE Credits Online.

How to Enroll

  1. Go to our Washington State approved courses page
  2. Browse courses — if you need an Equity approved course, use the subject filter to find it
  3. On the course page, use the Type dropdown: Course Only — includes your Certificate of Completion, which you'll submit for clock hours University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.
  4. Add the course to your cart and check out
  5. You will be asked to log in or create an account
  6. If you have a discount code, enter it at the end of checkout
  7. Upon purchase you will receive an email receipt and can log in to your course at eClassroom Sign in
  • Course Only — includes your Certificate of Completion, which you'll submit for clock hours
  • University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course.
