How to get your Wyoming PTSB Renewal Units with CE Credits Online

CE Credits Online is a Wyoming PTSB-approved provider offering self-paced online courses for Renewal Units and Graduate-Level Professional Development Credits.

CE Credits Online is a partner of the Wyoming Professional Teachers Standards Board (PTSB). Our self-paced online courses are designed to help Wyoming teachers earn Renewal Units and Graduate-Level Professional Development Credits on their own schedule, 24/7.

Quick Answer

To earn Wyoming PTSB Renewal Units with CE Credits Online:

  • Successfully complete your course
  • CE Credits Online reports your completion directly to WY PTSB within 10 days — you don't have to do anything
  • You will need your WY PTSB ID Number for reporting purposes

Once you complete your course, we take care of the reporting for you.

How to Get Your Wyoming PTSB Renewal Units

Once you successfully complete your course, we'll email you your Certificate of Completion. CE Credits Online will then report your completion to the WY PTSB within 10 days.

If you have questions about Renewal Units, contact the WY PTSB directly at ptsbworkshoprequests@wyo.gov. Useful PTSB resources:

  • Renewal Requirements — Professional Development & Renewal Credit
  • Professional Development Approved Workshops

University Credits

If you need an official university transcript, you can add university credits when you purchase. Once you complete your course, we take care of the rest — we send all the required documentation directly to your chosen university, and they'll issue your official transcript and email you instructions for accessing it. Your transcript will include the course name, course number, number of credits, and your grade.

Graduate-level credits are available through Adams State University, Augustana University, Valley City State University, and Cal Poly Humboldt. Visit our University Partners page to learn more.

Wyoming PTSB Program Rules

  • CE Credits Online reports your completion to WY PTSB within 10 days — no paperwork required from you
  • You will need your WY PTSB ID Number for PTSB reporting
  • You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course
  • University credits must be added at purchase if needed

Wyoming PTSB FAQs

Does CE Credits Online report my completion to WY PTSB?

Yes. Once you successfully complete your course, CE Credits Online reports your completion directly to WY PTSB within 10 days. You don't need to submit any paperwork.

Where do I find my WY PTSB ID Number?

Your WY PTSB ID Number is available through your PTSB account. You will need it for reporting purposes after your purchase.

How long does it take to receive my Renewal Units?

CE Credits Online submits your completion to WY PTSB within 10 days of finishing your course. Processing time after that is determined by PTSB.

Do I need an official transcript for Wyoming PTSB Renewal Units?

No. Your Certificate of Completion and CE Credits Online's direct reporting to PTSB is all that's required for Renewal Units. If you also need a university transcript for other purposes, you can add university credits at purchase.

How long do I have to complete my course?

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from the date of purchase to complete your course at your own pace.

Who do I contact if I have questions about my Renewal Units?

For questions about Renewal Unit requirements or processing, contact WY PTSB at ptsbworkshoprequests@wyo.gov. For questions about your course or enrollment, contact CE Credits Online.

How to Enroll

After enrolling, WY PTSB teachers will be asked to provide their WY PTSB ID Number.

  1. Go to our Wyoming PTSB courses page
  2. Select the course or courses you'd like to take
  3. On the course page, use the Type dropdown: PTSB Renewal Units — includes your Certificate of Completion and PTSB reporting University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.
  4. Add the course to your cart and check out
  5. You will be asked to log in or create an account
  6. If you have a discount code, enter it at the end of checkout
  7. Upon purchase you will receive an email receipt and can log in to your course at eClassroom Sign in
  • PTSB Renewal Units — includes your Certificate of Completion and PTSB reporting
  • University Credits — adds an official university transcript. A second dropdown will appear to choose your university partner.

You have 180 days (about 6 months) from purchase to complete your course.
