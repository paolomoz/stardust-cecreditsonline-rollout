How to get your Pre-Approved LAUSD Salary Points

Earn LAUSD salary points with CE Credits Online. Our courses are JSPCC pre-approved for LAUSD school teachers, and we submit the required paperwork on your behalf.

  • LAUSD Independent Charter Schools

CE Credits Online courses are pre-approved by the JSPCC (Joint Salary Point Credit Committee), the body that oversees salary point approvals for LAUSD. Salary points count toward advancement on the LAUSD salary table — completing as few as 3–6 courses can move a teacher to the next salary level. We submit all required paperwork directly to LAUSD's Professional Development Unit after you finish your course.

Below, you’ll find everything you need to know about earning your LAUSD salary points and enrolling in a course.

Thousands of teachers have advanced their careers with CE Credits Online. See what they say →

Quick answer: To earn LAUSD salary points, enroll in a JSPCC pre-approved course, complete it within 180 days, and CE Credits Online will submit your paperwork to LAUSD for processing.

This guide covers:

  • How salary points are awarded
  • How to enroll in courses
  • What to expect after completion

CE Credits Online allows teachers to submit up to 12 salary points per trimester (Jan–Apr, May–Aug, Sept–Dec).

Step-by-step process

  • Successfully Complete your courseFinish your CE Credits Online course at your own pace. You have up to 180 days (about 6 months) to complete your course.
  • We submit your paperworkAfter completion, CE Credits Online submits your paperwork directly to the LAUSD Professional Development Unit, including the required NA Claim for Staff Development Point Project form. You do not need to take any additional action.
  • LAUSD awards your salary pointsThe LAUSD Professional Development Unit reviews your submission and awards salary points to your account.

Important things to know

  • No additional paperwork is required from you.Please do not submit anything separately—this can delay or interfere with processing.
  • Processing can take up to 3 months.Salary points are typically posted and show as "Pending" on your MyPLN account within 4 weeks but may take up to 3 months to be issued depending on LAUSD processing times.
  • Points are backdated to your completion date.Even if processing takes time, your salary advancement date will not be affected.
  • Your certificate of completion is for your records only.You do not need to submit it to LAUSD.
  • We recommend checking your salary point balance before and after completion.Check your current total before starting your course, then check again about 3 months after your completion paperwork has been submitted. LAUSD does not identify which coursework added the points, so this helps you confirm when your new salary points are posted.

Common questions

Do I need to submit paperwork to LAUSD myself?

No. CE Credits Online submits all required paperwork on your behalf to LAUSD.

How long does it take to receive LAUSD salary points?

Most salary points are posted within 4 weeks, though they may take up to 3 months to be issued, depending on LAUSD processing.

How many salary points can I apply per trimester?

CE Credits Online allows teachers to submit up to 12 salary points per trimester (Jan–Apr, May–Aug, Sept–Dec).

What happens if I enter my LAUSD information late?

Your paperwork may be delayed. We recommend adding your employee ID and school information as soon as you enroll.

Optional: Earning university credit

All courses are eligible for graduate-level professional development credit through our university partners. This option is not required for LAUSD salary points and is typically only needed if you plan to use your credits outside of LAUSD.

If you would like university credit, email us within 30 days of the completion of your course with:

  • The course you completed
  • Your completion confirmation
  • Your preferred university (from our University Partners)

Our team will guide you through the next steps to request your university credit.
