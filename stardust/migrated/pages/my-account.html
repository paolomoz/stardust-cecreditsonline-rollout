Here are some important details you should know:

Thank you for choosing CE Credits Online! We value your commitment to continuous learning and are here to help you make the most of your courses.

Please note that extensions are available for purchase after the status of the course has changed to "Expired". Courses expire 180 days (6 months) after the date of purchase. Should your course reach its expiration date, don't worry – you have the option to purchase extensions directly from this page. Your expiration date can be found next to your courses below. For a 3-credit course, a 30-day extension costs $50, for a 1 or 2 credit course, it is $30. You are free to extend your course as many times as needed after it has expired (following the initial 180 days). Please be aware that 12 months after the date of purchase, courses are discontinued if not completed, and extension options are no longer available. We recommend planning your schedule accordingly to benefit fully from the courses you purchase.

PLEASE NOTE: Extensions are not available to NYC DOE ASPDP participants, as the term dates are set by the ASPDP, and courses must be completed by the end of the term.

If you have any questions, please email us at support@cecreditsonline.org.
