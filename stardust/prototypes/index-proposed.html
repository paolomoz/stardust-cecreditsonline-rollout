About

What is CE Credits Online?

For more than 20 years, CE Credits Online has been helping K-12 teachers grow — on their schedule, at their own pace, and in ways that actually matter in the classroom.

Founded in 2001, CE Credits Online is a woman-owned company built around one simple idea: professional development should be worth a teacher's time. Our graduate-level courses are completely online and self-paced.
